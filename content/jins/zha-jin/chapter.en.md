## 炸劲 · zhà jìn

### The Deflagration Force

---

> Like a grenade exploding in all directions.

— Traditional saying

---

## The Essence of Zhà Jìn

Zhà means to explode, to burst, to detonate.

Unlike Bào Jìn, which is a directed explosion, Zhà Jìn is a spherical deflagration. It emits in all directions simultaneously.

The masters describe it as:

· a sudden expansion of the entire body,

· a force without privileged direction,

· a shock wave at three hundred and sixty degrees.

---

## Fundamental Principles

1\. The explosion is spherical

Zhà Jìn does not choose a direction. It emits everywhere at once: forward, backward, upward, downward, sideways. The body becomes the center of a deflagration.

2\. Every part of the body emits

The hands, the elbows, the shoulders, the back, the hips, the knees: every point of the body becomes a gun muzzle. The energy does not pass through a single channel; it takes them all.

3\. Compression precedes expansion

Zhà Jìn is born of a prior condensation. The body gathers, compacts, then explodes outward. It is the rhythm of the lung: inhalation, then thunderous expansion.

4\. The opponent does not know where the blow comes from

Because the force comes from everywhere, the opponent cannot identify its source. He is struck from all sides at the same time. His defense becomes impossible.

---

## Final Summary

Zhà Jìn is the force of total expansion. It does not pierce a point, it does not traverse a line. It fills all space in an instant, like a star being born.
