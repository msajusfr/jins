## 爆劲 · bào jìn

### The Explosive Force

---

> Like gunpowder catching fire.

— Traditional saying

---

## The Essence of Bào Jìn

Bào means to explode, to burst, to detonate.

Bào Jìn is a sudden, brief, total emission. It gives no warning. It does not build up gradually. It explodes.

The masters describe it as:

· an instantaneous release of accumulated energy,

· a force that knows no transition,

· a passage from zero to maximum without interval.

---

## Fundamental Principles

1\. The explosion is born of release

The paradox of Bào Jìn: the more the body is released before emission, the more violent the explosion. Preliminary tension smothers the detonation. Release liberates it.

2\. Zero time

Between silence and explosion, there is nothing. No signal, no swelling, no recoil. Bào Jìn is the incarnation of the instantaneous.

3\. The entire body participates

The explosion is not localized. It engages simultaneously the feet, the legs, the waist, the back, the arms. Every part of the body emits at the same instant.

4\. After the explosion, the void

Bào Jìn dies as quickly as it appears. It does not last. After emission, the body returns immediately to release.

---

## Final Summary

Bào Jìn is the force of the pure instant. It knows neither transition nor duration. It is the lightning that strikes before one has seen the flash.
