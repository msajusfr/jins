## 贯劲 · guàn jìn

### The Piercing Force

---

> Pierce like the needle through cloth.

— Traditional saying

---

## The Essence of Guàn Jìn

Guàn means to pass through from end to end, to penetrate, to connect.

Guàn Jìn is the force that traverses the target, that does not stop at its surface, that penetrates deeply.

The masters describe it as:

· a needle that passes through several layers,

· a wave that traverses the opponent's body,

· a vibration that propagates beyond the point of impact.

---

## Fundamental Principles

1\. Intention pierces first

The mind does not aim at the opponent's surface, but at a point beyond. Intention pierces first; the body follows.

2\. The force is fine and concentrated

Guàn Jìn is not a wide push. It is a punctual, dense penetration that passes through a small point to reach the interior.

3\. Vibration accompanies the penetration

Often, Guàn Jìn is carried by a subtle vibration, an oscillation that helps the force traverse the layers of resistance.

4\. The impact is internal

The opponent does not feel only surface pressure. He feels something that enters, that traverses, that touches the inside of his body.

---

## Final Summary

Guàn Jìn is the art of traversing. It does not stop at the skin, the muscles, the bones. It goes beyond, carried by fine, penetrating intention, like the needle that passes without tearing.
